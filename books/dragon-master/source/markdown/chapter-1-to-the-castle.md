# Chapter 1 - To The Castle!

## Page 8

TO THE CASTLE! rake doesn't see the king's soldier coming. He is busy digging in the onion patch. He pulls out a fat, white onion. A worm crawls on it. Drake doesn't mind the worm. He is the son of a farmer. His family has been growing onions in the Kingdom of Bracken forever. He will spend his life digging up onions, whether he wants to or not.

## Page 9

Drake picks up the worm. "Hello, little guy," he says. Then he puts the worm back in the dirt. "Are you Drake?" a loud voice asks behind him. Drake jumps and turns around. One of the king's soldiers ride up on a black horse. He has a yellow beard. His shirt has a gold dragon sewn on it - the symbol of King Roland the Bold. "Yes, I'm Drake," Drake says, his voice shaking. Soldiers never come to the fields. Not unless a farmer is in trouble.

## Page 10

The soldier rides up to Drake. He reaches down and pulls him up onto his horse. "Hey, what are you doing?" Drake yells. The soldier doesn't answer. Drake's mother runs out of their hut. "Wait! Where are you taking my boy?" she yells. "To King Roland," the soldier says. Drake's heart jumps a little. He has always wants to meet the king. "But he's only eight!" his mother yells. She marches up to the horse.

## Page 11

"The king has chosen him," the soldier says. Chosen me for what? Drake wonders. He knows better than to ask the soldier questions. Peasants like Drake aren't allowed to speak unless spoken to. "The king will take good care of him," the soldier says. Then he spurs on the horse, and speeds off. "Drake, do as the king says!" his mother calls out. Drake has never been on a horse before. He holds on tight. Drake's heart pumps fast as they race through the village. They race over the stone bridge. Finally, they stop in front of King Roland's castle.

## Page 13

The soldier helps Drake climb down from the horse. He opens the castle door and gives Drake a shove. They pass paintings and statues and people in fancy clothes. The soldier stomps behind Drake as they walks through the halls. Drake wants to look at everything, but the soldier gives him a push whenever he slows down. Then they come to some stairs. They walks down... down... down. The soldier stops at a door. "Where are we going?" Drake finally asks. "We are not going anywhere," the soldier says. "Good luck." Then he runs back up the stairs. "Hey! What do you mean?" Drake yells. But he is all alone.

## Page 14

Drake looks at the big, stone door. He feels afraid. But more than that, he is curious. He pushes it open and sees... ... the face of a giant, red dragon! Drake blinks. He doesn't believe his eyes. Then- whoosh! - the dragon shoots a huge fireball from his mouth!
