Bo draws a capital Dand a lowercase d. "See?" Bo says. "The big D looks like a dragon with a big belly." He draws a picture on the paper. "Like Vulcan," Drake says with a laugh. Bo laughs, too. Moonlight glints off Bo's Dragon Stone. It reminds Drake of something he'd wants to ask Bo about. "Does your stone ever glow?" Drake asks. Bo shakes his head. "No," he says. "Why do you ask?"
