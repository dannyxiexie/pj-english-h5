# Chapter 8 - Flying Practice

## Page 48

FLYING PRACTICE After breakfast the next morning, Drake goes back underground with Griffith and the other Dragon Masters. "Why do we train all the way down here?" Drake asks as they walk.

## Page 49

"Don't you know?" Rori says. "We're a secret. No one knows the dragons are here. No one knows the Dragon Stone is real. And no one knows about us." Drake looks at the wizard. "It is true," Griffith says. "The king does not want others to know about the dragons." "Because he's building a dragon army?" Drake asks. "That is the king's business, not ours," say Griffith. He opens the door to the hallway of dragon caves. "Collect your dragons. We are going outside today." "Hooray!" yells Ana, Rori, and Bo.

## Page 50

"But won't someone see us if we go outside?" asks Drake. "No, we'll be hidden in the Valley of Clouds," says Ana. "Hurry, go get Worm!" Drake runs through the winding hallway to Worm's cave. Worm raises his head and looks at him. It reminds Drake of his nightmare. He shivers. "Come on, Worm. We are going outside," Drake says, as he opens the gate. Worm crawls out of his cave. Griffith leads them all down a dark tunnel.

## Page 51

The tunnel opens up into a bright field of grass. Tall hills rise up on all sides. "The sun!" cheers Ana. She twirls around. Drake looks up at the sun and smiles. "So what do we do out here?" he asks Ana grins. "We fly," she says. She pats Kepri on the head. "Show him, girl." Kepri raises her long neck and flies straight up. She loops and swirls in the air. Drake watches her, shading his eyes from the sun. He has never seen anything like it.

## Page 52

"Wait till you see Vulcan fly," Rori says. "Vulcan! Circle!" Vulcan flaps his big, red wings. He flies up into the sky and circles the field. "Wow!" Drake says. He looks over at Bo. "Your dragon doesn't have wings, but can she fly, too?" he asks. Bo nods. "She does not need wings," he says. "Shu, please fly." Shu floats up off the grass.

## Page 53

"It's like she's swimming through the air," Drake says. "Yes," says Bo. "How Shu flies is very much like how one swims. She can ride the winds." Drake looks at Worm. His tiny wings do not look like they can lift him. "What about you, Worm?" he asks. "Can you fly, too?"

## Page 54

Worm just stares at Drake. He doesn't flap his wings. He doesn't even move. "It's okay," Drake says. He thinks of how scared Worm has been in the nightmare. "We can watch the others." Drake sits on the grass. He puts a hand on Worm's back. The dragon moves a little closer to him. Suddenly, the Dragon Stone feels warm on Drake's skin. He looks down. It is glowing! Drake looks around. Griffith is standing over with the other Dragon Masters. None of their stones are glowing. Why is my stone glowing? he wonders. Am I doing something wrong?

## Page 55

Drake quickly tucks his Dragon Stone inside his shirt. Then he goes back to watching the other dragons fly across the sky.
