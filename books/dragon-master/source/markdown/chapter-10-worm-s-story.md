# Chapter 10 - Worm's Story

## Page 61

WORM'S STORY rake steps inside Worm's dark cave. Worm opens one eye. "I need to shine your scales," Drake says. He is carrying a brush, a basket, and towels. Drake looks at Worm's brown scales. "They're not shiny," he says. "But I'll clean them anyway."

## Page 62

Drake is still getting uses to being around Worm. The dragon's head is as big as Drake. Worm can swallow Drake in one gulp if he wants to. But something about Worm makes Drake feel... peaceful. Drake gently brushes one of Worm's scales. The big dragon makes a sound low in his throat. Worm smiles and closes his eyes.

## Page 63

"You like that?" Drake asks. Worm makes another purring sound. "Good." He cleans Worm's scales, one at a time. "I kind of miss the onion field back home," Drake says to his dragon. "It is hard work. But I love being outside." Drake starts to clean Worm's head. "And I really miss my family," Drake says.

## Page 64

He scratches behind Worm's ears, like he does with his cat back home. Then he feels his hand start tingling.... He tries to take his hand off Worm, but he cann't. It is stuck. Drake's eyes widen. He looks at Worm. The dragon is staring hard at him.

## Page 65

Pictures pop into Drake's head. He sees the cave from his nightmare. He sees the explosion again. Before, Drake has woken up. This time, the pictures keep coming....

## Page 66

Worm is trying to get out of the cave, but the other dragons are in the way. Then soldiers rush into the cave. Each soldier's shirt has a gold dragon sewn on it. "The king's soldiers?" Drake asks. The soldiers wrap Worm in chains. They drags him out of the cave. Owwwreeeeee! Drake can hear Worm's cry. Then his hand stops tingling. The pictures leave his head.

## Page 67

Drake looks at Worm. "Does that really happen? Does the king's men take you away from your family? Just like they take me away from mine?" Worm nods. "I'm so sorry," Drake says. He throws his arms around Worm's neck. Worm closes his eyes. All his life, Drake has looked up to King Roland. But why will the king's men treat Worm like a prisoner? wonders Drake. Maybe he isn't such a good king after all.
