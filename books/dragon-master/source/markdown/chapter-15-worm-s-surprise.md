# Chapter 15 - Worm's Surprise

## Page 88

WORM'S SURPRISE Worm didhit explode. Instead, the dragon closes his eyes. Then the rocks blocking the tunnel begins to shake. "What's happening?!" Bo yells. "Is Worm doing that?" asks Ana.

## Page 89

"I think ... I think he's using the power of his mind," Drake says. He isn't sure how he knows. He just does. Rori, Ana, and Bo steps back. The rocks keeps shaking. Then... Poof! The rocks break up into tiny pieces. Rock dust fills the air. Drake coughs, waving the dust away with his hand. All of the fallen rocks are gone. The tunnel is clear again!

## Page 90
