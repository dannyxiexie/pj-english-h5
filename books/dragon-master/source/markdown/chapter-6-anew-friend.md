# Chapter 6 - Anew Friend

## Page 37

ANEW FRIEND here is so much food on the dining room table! Roast chicken. Potatoes. Carrots. Bread. Cheese. It is more food than Drake has seen in his life. "Pass the potatoes, please," Drake says. Griffith points to the plate of potatoes. Sparks fly from his finger.

## Page 38

As the plate floats over to Drake, he forgets all about wanting to go home. He stabs a potato with his fork. "Is every supper like this?" Drake asks Bo. Bo nods. "Yes, there is always a great deal of good food," he says. "But sometimes I miss my mother's soup." "Is your home kingdom far away?" Drake asks. "Very far," says Bo. "I come from the east - the kingdom of Emperor Song."

## Page 39

"And I come from the south," adds Ana. "It is warm there. Not cold and damp like it is here." "Well, I am proud to be from this kingdom," Rori says. "My father is a blacksmith. He makes the best horseshoes in our village." Drake misses home. He turns to Griffith. "Is there a way I can let my family know I'm okay?" Drake asks. The wizard nods. "You may send them a note." He waves to one of the servants. "Please bring this boy paper and a quill." A servant gives Drake paper, a small pot of ink, and a feather. Drake's cheeks turns red. "What?" Rori asks. "Haven't you seen paper before?"

## Page 40

Drake looks down at his plate. "I work in the fields at home," he says. "I never go to school. I know how to reads. But we never have paper. Or quills. So I don't know how to write." Rori starts to say something, but Griffith gives her a hard look. Bo picks up the quill. "I will write the note for you," he says. "Thanks," Drake says. Then he tells Bo what to write. Dear Mother, I am safe so please do not worry. Everything is new and exciting. The king is keeping me well fed. My new friend Bo helps me write this letter. Love, Drake

## Page 41

Drake does not say anything about dragons. He thinks that might scare his mother. The servant takes the note away. Drake yawns. "So, where do we sleep?" "We have rooms in the tower," Bo says. "You will be in my room." Drake smiles. "Good." At least he has one new friend here. Suddenly, a soldier stomps into the room. "All rise for King Roland the Bold!" he says.
