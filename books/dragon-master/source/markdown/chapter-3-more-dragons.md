# Chapter 3 - More Dragons!

## Page 22

MORE DRAGONS! rake's mind is spinning as he leaves the wizard's workshop. He follows Griffith to a big underground room. There are no windows. Torches hung on the walls. Drake sees Rori and Vulcan. He also sees two more kids - and two more dragons!

## Page 23

"Drake, this is Bo," Griffith says, pointing to a boy with black hair, "and his dragon, Shu." Bo is petting his dragon's tail. This dragon has shiny blue scales, but no wings. "Hi," Drake says. "Nice to meet you, Drake," the boy says politely. Griffith leads Drake over to a girl with long, black hair. Her dragon has white scales. A yellow band of scales circle the dragon's neck. The tips of its wings are yellow, too.

## Page 24

"This is Ana, and her dragon, Kepri," Griffith says. Drake nods. "Hi," he says. "We can use somebody new around here," Ana says with a grin. "You have met the other Dragon Masters and their dragons. It is time for you to meet your dragon," Griffith says. Drake's heart pounds. My family will never believe this! he thinks. Just this morning I is digging onions. Now I have ridden a horse. I've meet a wizard. I've seen the Dragon Stone. And I'm going to have my very own dragon?!

## Page 25

He and the other Dragon Masters follow Griffith down another dark hallway. "The dragons sleep in caves when they're not training." Bo explains as they walk. "Vulcan's cave is the biggest," brags Rori. Griffith stops in front of a small cave. Wood bars cover the cave. "Drake, meet your dragon!" the wizard says.
