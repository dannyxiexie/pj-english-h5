# Chapter 7 - A Strange Dream

## Page 42

A STRANGE DREAM he Dragon Masters jump to their feet. King Roland sweeps into the room. He is a big man, with red hair and a bushy beard. He walks right up to Drake. Drake is so nervous, he is shaking.

## Page 43

"This is my new Dragon Master?" he asks. "Yes, Your Highness," say Griffith. The king frowns. "He's scrawny." Drake feels like sinking into the floor. The king turns to Griffith. "Explain this to me, wizard. My strongest men cannot control dragons. Why is it that these children can?" "It is the way of the Dragon Stone," Griffith says. "It is a mystery, even to me." "Hmph!" snorts the king. "Very well, then. I shall leave my army in your hands." Army? Drake wonders.

## Page 44

King Roland looks at Drake again. "Do not lets me down, boy" he says. Then he and the soldier leaves. Everyone sits back down. The king's words scare Drake. What will happen if I do let him down? Drake wonders. He has a feeling it willn't be good. Bo takes Drake to their room. There is a bed and a wooden chest for each of them. There is also a desk for them to share. A jug of water sits on a table. "That is your bed," Bo says, pointing. Drake climbs into bed.

## Page 45

The moon shone through a small window. Drake looks over at Bo. He is already sleeping peacefully. Drake soon drifts off to Sleep, too. All of the sudden, he is in a dark cave.

## Page 46

The air in the cave feels warm. It smells like the deep, rich dirt where the onions grow. Green eyes glow in the darkness. Worm! Worm is in the cave. And behind him are other dragons. They all has the same brown scales and green eyes.

## Page 47

Boom! A loud explosion shakes the cave. Smoke fills the air. Worm lets out a cry. The dragons slither across the cave floor, looking for a way out. Drake wakes up in his bed, dripping sweat. That is some nightmare, he thinks. It feels so real....
